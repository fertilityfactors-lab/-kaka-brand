Brand Story

The heart behind the pearl

"
Ours is a brand from my Ummachi's heart to my little brain. I planted it, nurtured it and farmed it until it turned to a big tree from which hundreds of other mothers can eat its fruit.

— Founder, Kaka

The Origin

Kaka was born from an Ummachi's (grandmother's) love for pearls — a love that began as fascination, grew into obsession, and blossomed into a lifelong passion. What started as admiration for the ocean's most elegant gem became the seed of a brand that would empower hundreds of women.

The Vision

Pearl can elevate our looks. From simple everyday wear to grand wedding ensembles, it takes us to the next level. The vision behind Kaka is simple yet profound — every woman must feel that elegance at least once in her lifetime. Each piece is made by a woman, for a woman.

The Name

Kaka — derived from the deep-rooted heritage of Kerala, carries the warmth of home and the strength of tradition. "Ama" means "Woman of the Sea" — the pearl divers, the mothers, the creators. Together, they represent a brand that is as natural and precious as the pearls it crafts.

Heritage Connection

Rooted in Kerala's rich cultural heritage, Kaka bridges tradition with contemporary elegance. Each piece carries the story of generations — from the pearl-diving communities of the coast to the artisan women who shape every ornament with their hands and hearts.

Brand Strategy

Purpose, positioning & promise

Mission

To deliver uncompromising quality and prompt delivery in every handcrafted pearl piece, making premium elegance accessible to every woman.

Vision

To open an exclusive showroom for pearls. Expanding into lab-grown diamonds, amethyst stones, and precious stones. Customising each piece as per client's requirements. Building a brand with hundreds of women workers.

Core Purpose

Beyond selling products — to build a sustainable ecosystem empowering hundreds of women artisans, creating livelihood through the art of pearl jewellery making.

Unique Selling Proposition

Each piece is handmade from AAA Quality freshwater pearls and the rarest South Sea pearls — crafted by women, for women. An experience of elegance that anyone can afford.

Logo System

The visual cornerstone of our identity

Primary Logo — Symbol + Text

The primary logo should be used in most brand applications. Always maintain clear space equal to the height of the "K" character around all sides.

Logo Variations

Icon Mark

Use for favicons, social media profile pictures, and small-scale applications.

Monogram — AK

For embossing, watermarks, packaging seals, and premium stationery.

Light Version

For use on dark backgrounds, photography overlays, and dark-themed materials.

Gold Foil Version

For premium packaging, foil stamping, and luxury print applications.

Clear Space & Minimum Size

KAKA
X X X X

Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space of "X" around the logo, where X equals the height of the letter "K" in the wordmark. No other elements should encroach this zone.

Print 25mm
Digital 120px

Minimum Size

To maintain legibility, never reproduce the logo smaller than 25mm wide for print or 120px wide for digital applications.

Imagery & Art Direction

Visual language that tells our story

Brand Moodboard

Photography Style

Product Photography

  • Clean, minimalist backgrounds in Pearl White or Midnight Navy
  • Macro shots highlighting pearl lustre and texture
  • Soft, diffused natural lighting — never harsh direct light
  • Show craftsmanship details — clasps, settings, threading
  • Use silk or linen fabric as props — never plastic or synthetic materials

Lifestyle & Model Photography

  • Natural, warm tones with golden hour lighting preferred
  • Models should reflect diverse Indian beauty — all skin tones
  • Settings: heritage homes, ocean backdrops, minimalist interiors
  • Emotional storytelling — capture moments, not just products
  • Model shoots + lifestyle storytelling as primary content style

Color Grading

  • Warm undertones — never cool or blue-tinted
  • Slightly desaturated for editorial elegance
  • Rich shadows, soft highlights
  • Consistent filter/grade across all platforms

Forbidden Styles

  • No harsh flash photography
  • No overly filtered or Instagram-trendy effects
  • No cluttered or busy backgrounds
  • No stock photography — always original content

Tone of Communication

Primary Tone: Emotional

Our communication should touch hearts. We speak about heritage, love, family, and the joy of adorning oneself. Every caption, every description should feel like it's written from a mother's heart.

✓ Example

"Born from an Ummachi's love, crafted by women who understand your journey — this isn't just jewellery, it's a piece of home you carry with you."

Supporting: Premium

While emotional at core, our language should reflect premium quality. Use refined vocabulary without being pretentious.

✓ Example

"Handcrafted from AAA-grade South Sea pearls, each piece undergoes 12 quality checks before it reaches you."

Language Guidelines

Bilingual Approach

Content should be a natural mix of English and Malayalam, reflecting the brand's Kerala heritage while maintaining international appeal.

✓ Do Say

  • "Handcrafted with love"
  • "Elegance you can feel"
  • "From our home to yours"
  • "Made by women, for women"
  • "Heritage meets modern grace"

✗ Don't Say

  • "Cheap" or "affordable" (use "accessible luxury")
  • "Buy now!" or aggressive sales language
  • "Imitation" or "artificial"
  • "Limited stock!" — avoid urgency tactics
  • Overly casual slang

Social Media Guidelines

Instagram

Grid Aesthetic

Alternate between product shots, lifestyle images, and text-based quotes. Maintain warm, cohesive color grading throughout.

Stories

Behind-the-scenes crafting process, artisan spotlights, customer testimonials. Use branded story templates.

Reels

Model shoots, lifestyle storytelling, pearl education, unboxing reveals. Warm, emotional background music.

Emotional, storytelling approach. Mix of English and Malayalam. Always include brand hashtags.

Design Style

Rich and premium feel. Large hero imagery, elegant typography, smooth scroll animations. Midnight Navy & Gold primary scheme.

Product Pages

High-res images with zoom. Include pearl type, size, material details. Storytelling product descriptions.
